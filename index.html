About Us

Born from the Streets,
Refined for the Future

La Vague emerged from a simple belief: streetwear should be timeless. Founded in 2024, we set out to create pieces that transcend seasons and trends — garments that feel as relevant today as they will in a decade.

Every piece is thoughtfully designed in our studio and crafted in Nigeria using premium materials. We don't chase hype. We build classics.
