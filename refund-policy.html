Refund Policy

Our commitment to fair and transparent refunds

Last Updated: February 14, 2024

Our Promise: We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy with your order, we'll make it right through a refund, exchange, or store credit.

1. Overview

LA VAGUE offers refunds under specific conditions outlined in this policy. By making a purchase, you agree to the terms of this Refund Policy. This policy works alongside our Returns Policy, which covers the process for returning items.

2. Refund Eligibility

2.1 Eligible for Full Refund

  • Items returned within 14 days of delivery
  • Items in original condition with tags attached
  • Items that are unworn, unwashed, and unaltered
  • Defective or damaged items (with photo evidence)
  • Incorrect items shipped
  • Items that never arrived (after investigation)

2.2 Eligible for Partial Refund

  • Items returned after 14 days but within 30 days (store credit only)
  • Items missing original packaging (up to 15% restocking fee)
  • Sale items (refund difference between sale price and current price)

2.3 Not Eligible for Refund

  • Items worn, washed, or altered
  • Items without original tags
  • Final sale items (marked as "No Returns")
  • Gift cards and store credits
  • Items damaged due to customer misuse
  • Intimate wear (for hygiene reasons)

3. Refund Process

4. Refund Methods

4.1 Original Payment Method

Refunds will be issued to the original payment method used for the purchase:

  • Credit/Debit Cards: 5-10 business days
  • Bank Transfer: 3-7 business days
  • Mobile Money: 1-3 business days

4.2 Store Credit

You may choose to receive store credit instead of a refund. Benefits include:

  • No restocking fees
  • Instant availability
  • Never expires
  • Can be used with other promotions

4.3 Exchange

Exchange for a different size or color is available at no additional cost, subject to inventory availability.

5. Refund Amounts

5.1 Full Refund

A full refund includes:

  • Item purchase price
  • Original shipping cost (if item is defective or incorrect)

5.2 Partial Refunds

Partial refunds may be issued for:

  • Late returns (store credit value only)
  • Items missing packaging (minus 15% restocking fee)
  • Sale items (current sale price)

5.3 Non-Refundable Items

The following are never refunded:

  • Return shipping costs (unless item is defective)
  • Gift wrapping fees
  • Express shipping upgrades (unless order was cancelled)

6. Special Circumstances

6.1 Defective Items

If you receive a defective item:

  • Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
  • Provide photos of the defect
  • We will provide a prepaid return label
  • Full refund including return shipping

6.2 Lost Packages

If your package is lost in transit:

  • We will investigate with the carrier
  • If confirmed lost, full refund or reshipment at your choice
  • Investigation may take 5-10 business days

6.3 Cancelled Orders

  • Orders cancelled before shipment: Full refund
  • Orders cancelled after shipment: Refund minus shipping costs
  • Custom orders cannot be cancelled once production begins

7. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund:

  1. Contact our support team at support@lavague.com
  2. Provide your order number and reason for refund
  3. Wait for return authorization (within 24 hours)
  4. Ship the item using the provided return label
  5. Receive confirmation when refund is processed

You can also initiate a return through your order tracking page if you have your order details.

8. Currency and Exchange Rates

Refunds are processed in the original currency of purchase. If you paid in:

  • NGN: Refund in NGN to your Nigerian account
  • USD: Refund in USD to your original card
  • Other currencies: Refund at current exchange rate (may differ from purchase rate)

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Refund Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date. Refund requests are processed according to the policy in effect at the time of your purchase.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about refunds or need assistance:

  • Email: support@lavague.com
  • Phone: Available on our Contact Page
  • Response time: Within 24 hours
