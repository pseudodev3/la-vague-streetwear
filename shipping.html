Common Questions

Do you ship internationally?

Currently we only ship within Nigeria. International shipping is coming soon - sign up for our newsletter to be notified!

What if I'm not home for delivery?

Our courier will attempt delivery twice. After the second attempt, your package will be held at a nearby pickup location for 5 days.

Can I change my shipping address?

Address changes must be made within 2 hours of placing your order. Contact us immediately at support@lavague.com.
