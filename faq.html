Returns & Exchanges

We accept returns within 30 days of delivery. Items must be unworn, unwashed, and have all original tags attached.

Contact us at returns@lavague.com with your order number. We'll send you a return label and instructions.

Refunds are processed within 5-7 business days after we receive your return. It may take an additional 2-5 business days for your bank to reflect the refund.

Sizing & Fit

Check our detailed size guide on each product page. Our pieces are designed with an oversized fit. If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing down for a more fitted look.

Our hoodies and tees have an intentional oversized fit. Bottoms run true to size. Check the product description for specific fit details.
