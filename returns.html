Return Policy

We want you to love your LA VAGUE pieces. If you're not completely satisfied, you can return any item within 30 days of delivery for a full refund or exchange.

Return Conditions:

  • Items must be unworn and unwashed
  • All original tags must be attached
  • Items must be in original packaging
  • Proof of purchase required

How to Return

1

Initiate Return

Email us at returns@lavague.com with your order number and reason for return.

2

Get Return Label

We'll send you a prepaid return label via email within 24 hours.

3

Package & Ship

Pack items securely with all tags attached. Drop off at any of our partner locations.

4

Receive Refund

Once received and inspected, your refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.

Exchange Policy

Need a different size or color? We've got you covered.

Size Exchanges

Exchange for a different size at no additional cost. If the new size costs more, we'll charge the difference. If it costs less, we'll refund the difference.

Color Exchanges

Swap for a different color in the same style. Subject to availability - if your preferred color is out of stock, we'll offer a refund or store credit.

Style Exchanges

Want a completely different item? Return your original item for a refund and place a new order for the item you want.

Note: Exchanges are processed as returns + new orders. This ensures you get your new item faster while your return is being processed.

Non-Returnable Items

For hygiene and safety reasons, the following items cannot be returned:

Intimates & Undergarments
Face Masks & Coverings
Items Marked "Final Sale"
Gift Cards & Store Credit
Worn or Washed Items
Damaged or Altered Items

Damaged or Defective Items

Received a damaged or defective item? We sincerely apologize and will make it right immediately.

  • Contact us within 7 days of delivery
  • Include photos of the defect or damage
  • We'll send a replacement at no cost
  • Or provide a full refund - your choice
